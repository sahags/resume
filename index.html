    3. Master's dissertation project in the Department of Chemistry, Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur)

      Sept'21-June'22
      Mentor: Dr.Nagma Parveen

      Abstract: To study the virus-receptor interaction in vitro, we used model membranes (liposomes, proteoliposomes, supported lipid bilayer). We started with simple dipalmitoylphosphatidylcholine (DOPC) lipid then increased its complexity by adding different lipids and cholesterol and finally incorporated the protein into it. We checked different parameters that affect the liposome formation as well as supported lipid bilayer formation. We also proposed the function of Bio-Beads SM-2 (nonpolar polystyrene absorbent) in the removal of detergent which eventually led us to construct the proteoliposome. Mimicking membranes- liposomes & proteoliposome could be a useful tool to study the virus-receptor interaction in a cell free environment.
